Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ current_date }}

Information We Collect

When you use Spotify Tools, we collect the following information through the Spotify Web API:

  • Your Spotify user profile information (display name, email)
  • Access to your playlists for reading and modification
  • Track metadata (artist names, song titles, release dates, album information)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide our playlist management services:

  • Sorting playlists by release date
  • Removing duplicate tracks from playlists
  • Authenticating your identity with Spotify

Data Storage and Security

  • We do not permanently store your playlist data or track information
  • Authentication tokens are stored temporarily in secure session storage
  • All data processing happens in real-time and is not retained after use
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information or music data to third parties. Your data is only used to communicate with Spotify's servers on your behalf to perform the requested actions.

Prohibited Uses

This application explicitly prohibits:

  • Using data for AI training or machine learning purposes
  • Downloading or storing copyrighted content
  • Bulk data extraction or scraping
  • Any commercial use of user data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Spotify account at any time through your Spotify settings
  • Request information about what data we access
  • Log out and clear your session data at any time

Spotify Web API

This application uses the Spotify Web API and is subject to Spotify's terms of service and privacy policy. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Spotify AB or any of its subsidiaries.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository.

Note: This privacy policy applies to this specific Spotify Tools application. Your use of Spotify itself is governed by Spotify's own privacy policy and terms of service.
